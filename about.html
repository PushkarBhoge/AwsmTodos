About AwsmTodos

A modern, secure, and user-friendly todo application designed to help you stay organized and productive.

PB

Pushkar Bhoge

Full Stack Developer & Creator

This website is created by Pushkar Bhoge with passion for clean design and user experience. Built with modern web technologies to provide you with the best todo management experience.

🔒

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your device. All todos are stored locally in your browser - no servers, no tracking, complete privacy.

💾

Local Storage

Your todos are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Close the app, restart your computer - your data stays safe.

📱

Responsive Design

Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Take your todos with you wherever you go.

🚀 Modern Technology

Built with vanilla JavaScript, modern CSS, and HTML5. No frameworks, no dependencies - just clean, efficient code that loads fast and works reliably.

🎨 Beautiful Design

Featuring a modern glass-morphism design with smooth animations, custom scrollbars, and a purple gradient theme that's easy on the eyes.

⚡ Fast & Lightweight

Optimized for performance with minimal code and efficient algorithms. No bloat, no unnecessary features - just what you need to manage your todos effectively.

🔧 Easy to Use

Intuitive interface with keyboard shortcuts, drag interactions, and clear visual feedback. Add, complete, and delete todos with just a few clicks.
